About Rebellion Cheer & Dance

Rebellion Allstars was founded by current head coach, Ellie Glastonbury in June 2018. Alongside a highly experienced and passionate coaching team, the club has grown rapidly and currently has over 150 members, aged 4-Adult. The programme competes in cheer competitions all over the UK, and more recently several members competed with Team Wales in the ICU World Championships in Orlando Florida! With an emphasis on fun, personal development and championing sportsmanship, the club has not only won many competitions but also taken part in several community events and fundraisers. For the 2022/2023 season, the club will continue to service the community by offering a school programme, community outreach camps and a brand new dance division.
