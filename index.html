Join the Rebellion!

Through Cheerleading, we put an emphasis on building a culture where our athletes learn that no matter what they do, their character defines who they are. This will help them reach their true potential as a cheerleaders and set them up for success in wherever their future may lead them. Founded in 2018 and led by coaches that have competed on the world stage, Rebellion Allstars works with young athletes to not only empower them but help them develop their skills on the floor. At RA athletes will be encouraged to express themselves, work hard, and not be afraid to make mistakes. Team culture, respect, discipline, hard work, honesty, and integrity are at the forefront of RA.
